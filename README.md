This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the Tour de France Femmes highlights from 2022 to 2025. It is designed to inform audiences about standout riders, team achievements, and race competitiveness through clear, interactive visualizations.
This enable fans, analysts, and stakeholders to Understand race outcomes at a glance, Spot long-term trends in rider and team performance, and Gain insights into competitiveness and consistency across seasons.

The goal of this dashboard is to:
1. Showcase standout riders and team achievements.
2. Compare how different rider groups finished relative to the winner.
3. Identify riders who dominated stage wins across multiple years.
4. Track team victories year by year and their cumulative success.
5. Help viewers quickly spot patterns in rider dominance, team consistency, and overall race competitiveness.

Key Features
1. Champion & Jersey Winners: Highlights of top performers each year.
2. Time Gap Analysis: Average time differences between rider groups (Top Contender, Chaser, Mid Rank, Backmarker).
3. Stage Dominance: Breakdown of stage wins by rider across four years.
4. Team Performance: Yearly and total wins by team, showing consistency and dominance.
5. Visual Storytelling: Engaging charts and tables that make trends easy to interpret.

Tools & Techniques
1. Power BI for interactive dashboard design.
2. Data Cleaning & Transformation using Power Query.
3. Data Modeling with relationships between tables.
4. DAX Measures for reusable calculations across dashboards.
5. Best Practices in naming conventions and structured data models for maintainability.
